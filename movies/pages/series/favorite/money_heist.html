    Summary:

    "Money Heist" (La Casa de Papel) is a Spanish heist crime drama television series that premiered in 2017. The show follows a group of robbers, led by a mastermind known as "The Professor," who plan and execute intricate heists on the Royal Mint of Spain and the Bank of Spain.

    The robbers take on code names based on cities, including Tokyo, Berlin, Nairobi, Denver, and others, to maintain anonymity during the heists. The Professor orchestrates the operations from the outside, while the team members carry out their roles inside the targeted buildings.

    Throughout the series, the narrative delves into the personal lives, emotions, and motivations of the robbers, as well as the negotiations and confrontations with law enforcement.

    "Money Heist" is known for its intense plot twists, suspenseful storytelling, and character development. The series has gained widespread international popularity and has become one of the most-watched non-English language shows on streaming platforms, captivating audiences with its thrilling heists and complex interpersonal relationships.
