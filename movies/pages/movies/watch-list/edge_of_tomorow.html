    Summary:

    "Edge of Tomorrow" is a science fiction action film released in 2014, directed by Doug Liman. The movie is based on the Japanese light novel "All You Need Is Kill" by Hiroshi Sakurazaka.

    The story is set in a future where Earth is invaded by an alien race called Mimics, and humanity is on the brink of extinction. Tom Cruise stars as William "Bill" Cage, a military officer with no combat experience, who finds himself thrust into the frontlines of the war against the Mimics.

    During a disastrous battle, Cage is killed, but he inexplicably wakes up to find himself reliving the previous day. Trapped in a time loop, he gains the ability to reset each day whenever he dies.

    Cage soon encounters Rita Vrataski, played by Emily Blunt, a skilled soldier renowned for her success in killing Mimics. With her help, Cage learns to harness his time-looping ability and gradually becomes a formidable warrior.

    As Cage and Rita work together to unravel the secret behind the Mimics' power and find a way to defeat them, they face numerous challenges and deadly encounters.

    "Edge of Tomorrow" is praised for its innovative storytelling, exhilarating action sequences, and the dynamic chemistry between Tom Cruise and Emily Blunt. The film combines elements of science fiction, time travel, and intense military action, making it a thrilling and engaging cinematic experience.
