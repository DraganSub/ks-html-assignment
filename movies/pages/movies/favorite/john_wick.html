    Summary:

    "John Wick" is an action-packed movie released in 2014, starring Keanu Reeves as the titular character. The story follows John Wick, a retired hitman seeking vengeance for the death of his beloved dog, which was a gift from his deceased wife. When a group of thugs led by a Russian mobster breaks into his home, steals his car, and kills his dog, they unknowingly unleash a relentless killing machine. John Wick goes on a brutal and relentless quest for revenge, leaving a trail of destruction and taking down anyone who stands in his way. The film is known for its intense action sequences, stylish visuals, and Keanu Reeves' iconic portrayal of the lethal and stoic hitman.
