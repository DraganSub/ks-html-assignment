    Plot:

    "The Silent Patient" is a gripping psychological thriller written by Alex Michaelides. The story follows Alicia Berenson, a famous artist who seemingly has a perfect life until she shoots her husband, Gabriel, and then goes silent, never speaking another word. She is committed to a psychiatric facility, and her case captures the public's imagination.

    Theo Faber, a criminal psychotherapist, becomes obsessed with Alicia's case and is determined to uncover the truth behind her silence. He takes a job at the same psychiatric facility to get closer to her. As Theo delves deeper into Alicia's past and psyche, he unravels dark secrets and unexpected twists, leading to a shocking revelation that changes everything. "The Silent Patient" is a suspenseful and mind-bending thriller that keeps readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.
