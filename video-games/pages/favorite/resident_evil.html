    Plot:

    "Resident Evil" is a survival horror video game franchise developed and published by Capcom. The series debuted in 1996 and has since become one of the most iconic and influential franchises in the horror genre.

    The games are set in various locations, often involving a fictional corporation known as the Umbrella Corporation, which conducts unethical and dangerous experiments with biological weapons, leading to the creation of deadly viruses that turn humans into mutated creatures.

    Players typically take on the roles of various characters, including special forces agents, police officers, and civilians, who find themselves trapped in nightmarish situations where they must survive and uncover the truth behind the viral outbreak.

    The gameplay of "Resident Evil" emphasizes exploration, puzzle-solving, and resource management, creating a tense and atmospheric experience. Players must confront hordes of zombies and other horrifying creatures, using limited ammunition and supplies to survive.

    Over the years, the series has evolved to include various gameplay styles, from the traditional survival horror to more action-oriented gameplay. The franchise has also expanded to include numerous spin-off games, movies, novels, and other media.

    "Resident Evil" is celebrated for its masterful use of horror elements, compelling storytelling, and memorable characters. The series has left a significant impact on the gaming industry and continues to captivate players with its terrifying and thrilling experiences.
